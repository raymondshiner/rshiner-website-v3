This site leans hard into the AI-assisted side of the workshop. Andromeda theme, MDX content layer, an Ask Raymond chat, three Claude Code subagents named after the crew. The rest of the portfolio shows you what that workflow can build.

This page is the other side of the bench.

I keep a separate file — a long list of things I want to write about. Some of them are short and sharp. Some are theses I've been circling for months. Most are honest enough that I'd rather draft them in private until they're ready, and then bring them here.

So `/writing` is going to come up slowly. One post at a time, when each one is actually done.
